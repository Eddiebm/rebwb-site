Term Sheet Examples

12-Unit Apartment Building • $1,200,000 Purchase Price

TERM SHEET — "THE GOOD"

First Capital Commercial Lending

BORROWER-FRIENDLY

Loan Terms

Property12-Unit Multifamily
Purchase Price$1,200,000
Loan Amount$900,000 (75% LTV)
Interest Rate6.25% Fixed
Loan Term5 Years + Two 1-Year Extensions
Amortization30 Years

Risk Factors

DSCR Requirement1.35x minimum
RecourseNon-Recourse (standard carve-outs)
Prepayment3-2-1 step-down
Rate Lock30 days at application
Reserves6 months PITI

Assessment: Strong equity cushion, healthy cash flow margin, personal assets protected, ample time flexibility with extensions, pricing certainty with 30-day lock.

TERM SHEET — "THE OKAY"

Regional Bank Commercial Division

PROCEED WITH CAUTION

Loan Terms

Property12-Unit Multifamily
Purchase Price$1,200,000
Loan Amount$960,000 (80% LTV)
Interest Rate6.50% Fixed
Loan Term3 Years + One 1-Year Extension
Amortization25 Years

Risk Factors

DSCR Requirement1.20x minimum
RecoursePartial (25% of loan balance)
Prepayment2% flat for 2 years
Rate Lock14 days at commitment
Reserves9 months PITI

Assessment: Workable but tighter margins. Less equity cushion, minimal cash flow buffer, partial personal exposure ($240K at risk), shorter runway, and rate uncertainty.

TERM SHEET — "THE BAD"

Hard Money Capital Partners

HIGH RISK — WALK AWAY

Loan Terms

Property12-Unit Multifamily
Purchase Price$1,200,000
Loan Amount$1,020,000 (85% LTV)
Interest Rate8.75% Variable
Loan Term2 Years, No Extensions
AmortizationInterest-Only

Risk Factors

DSCR Requirement1.10x minimum
RecourseFull Personal Guarantee
Prepayment5% flat, no decline
Rate LockNone — floating rate
Reserves12 months PITI

Assessment: Designed for the lender to win. Minimal equity cushion (15%), razor-thin cash flow margin, full personal exposure to all assets, no time flexibility, and complete rate uncertainty. Any market downturn, vacancy spike, or personal setback exposes you completely.
