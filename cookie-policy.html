Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Real Estate Without Bullsh*t™ uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and account access. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

  • Session management
  • Authentication and login status
  • Security features
  • Load balancing

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

  • Google Analytics (_ga, _gid, _gat)
  • Page view tracking
  • Traffic source analysis
  • User behavior patterns

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable personalized features and remember your preferences.

  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Language settings
  • Previously viewed content

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and performance
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Supabase: Authentication services

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from particular sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Browser-specific instructions:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

5. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, please note that:

  • Some features of the website may not function properly
  • You may not be able to access certain areas of the site
  • Your preferences may not be saved between visits
  • You may need to log in each time you visit

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
